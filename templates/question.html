Who Wants To Be a Millionaire?

During the first few months of the pandemic the UK saw an ever-growing demand for Personal Protective Equipment. As a government Minister for the Department of Health, you have been tasked with supplying the NHS with PPE to help keep their vital staff members safe whilst treating those suffering from COVID-19. In order to supply this essential PPE to doctors and nurses throughout the country, do you:

A: Choose a well-established company with experience producing and supplying PPE

B: Accept offers of help from companies who have volunteered their services during the pandemic

C: Utilise various local companies around the country in order to get the PPE to where it is needed as quickly as possible

D: Buy 400,000 gowns from a Turkish T-Shirt manufacturer
